Making Cooking Easier

Cooking is about timing, and conventional recipes make it difficult to keep all time-sensitive matters in mind. Cookoo solves this problem by creating an ordered and simple timeline that shows you when to start, when to prepare what and reminds you to preheat the oven in time. Try it out and see how easy timing can be.
